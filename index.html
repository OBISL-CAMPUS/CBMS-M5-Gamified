02 Step 2: Little's Law & Bottleneck Calibrator

Little's Law states that Work-in-Progress (WIP) = Arrival Rate (λ) × Flow Time (W). A bottleneck occurs where a workstation's processing capacity is lower than the arrival rate, causing queues to explode and utilization to exceed 100%.
